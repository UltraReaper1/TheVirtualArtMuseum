Exhibit D: Famous Art From Around the World

Exhibit D in the Atlanta Art Museum is the home of some of our most famous paintings from around the world. These paintings embody our principles, and display the various cultures from around the world. In this exhibit, it is easy for one to see the vast diveristy around the world through art. The age of art pieces in this exhibit vary vastly, as some paintings are very old and historic.
