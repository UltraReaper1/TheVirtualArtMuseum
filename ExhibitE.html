Exhibit E: Scultpures

Sculptures are some of the hardest pieces to create. Sculptures are generally carved from marble or another rock using hand tools, and this makes the process incredibly difficult. There are many sculptures from around the world, famously European Sculptures. These sculptures generally came from the Renaissance Period in Europe. These sculptures are said to be masterpieces now, and the Atlanta Art Musuem is proud to help display them to the world.
